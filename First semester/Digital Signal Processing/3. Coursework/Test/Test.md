# Wireless Communications Test Questions

## Question 1
A larger RMS delay spread means:

- a. Higher data rates
- b. No fading
- c. More ISI
- d. Higher SNR

**Correct Answer**: More ISI

---

## Question 2
Coherence bandwidth helps determine if:

- a. No Fading
- b. SNR is low
- c. Channel is flat
- d. Noise is high

**Correct Answer**: Channel is flat

---

## Question 3
Complete fading occurs when:

- a. Power increases
- b. No multipath exists
- c. Signals are in phase
- d. Signals are out of phase

**Correct Answer**: Signals are out of phase

---

## Question 5

## Question 21
